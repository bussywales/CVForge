# Glossary

- **Role Fit** – The deterministic scoring engine that compares your profile, achievements, and notes against the job description and highlights gaps.
- **Evidence Engine** – Suggests achievements or work-history bullets that match Role Fit gaps and lets you attach them to the CV, cover letter, or STAR drafts.
- **STAR Library** – Stores per-gap STAR drafts built from selected evidence; you can edit them and reuse them in Practice/Drill Mode.
- **Interview Pack** – Generates questions, prompts, and weak-spot drills aligned to the job description and Role Fit signals.
- **Practice Mode & Drill Mode** – Practice Mode gives you scores and rewrites; Drill Mode walks through low-score questions one-by-one.
- **Answer Pack** – Turns STAR drafts into polished Standard and 90-second answers you can copy into Practice drafts.
- **Smart Apply** – Submission readiness dashboard with checklist updates triggered by exports, kit downloads, and marked submissions.
- **Application Kit** – ZIP download of CV, cover letter, interview pack, and STAR JSON for submission-ready artefacts.
- **Outreach Engine** – Deterministic email/LinkedIn templates, logging, and next-step scheduling tracked via pipeline activities.
